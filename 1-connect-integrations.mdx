# Integrations Setup

Alright, now that we have your account set up, let's get your data flowing. We’re going to head over to the **Settings** menu and select **Integrations**. This is where you’ll connect your different data sources to sync your conversions, ads, and customer info.

## Getting Started

To access your integrations hub:

1. Navigate to the sidebar on the left.
2. Click on **Settings**.
3. Select the **Integrations** tab at the top of the page.

<CardGroup cols={2}><Card title="Native Integrations" icon="plug">We have 25+ native integrations ready to go, including CRMs, E-commerce stores, and booking software.</Card><Card title="Ad Networks" icon="chart-network">Connect your Meta, TikTok, and Google Ads accounts to optimize your campaigns and increase your ROI.</Card></CardGroup>

## Connecting Your Data Sources

It’s quite simple—just find the tool you use and click the **Connect** button. We support a wide range of platforms:

- **CRMs:** GoHighLevel, HubSpot, Salesforce.
- **E-commerce:** Shopify, WooCommerce, Whop.
- **Payments & Forms:** Stripe, Typeform, Tally Forms.
- **Scheduling:** Calendly, Cal.com.

<Note>**Coming from Hyros?** We have a specific **Hyros Data Import** tool. Just click **Import** to perform a one-time transfer of all your historical data and performance history.</Note>

## Setting Up Ad Tracking

Once you’ve connected your conversion sources, you’ll want to connect your marketing stack. Scroll down to find your ad platforms:

- **Meta Ads**
- **TikTok Ads**
- **Google Ads**
- **Bing Ads**

Connecting these allows us to send conversion data back to the platforms, helping your ads perform better at a lower cost.

## What if my app isn't listed?

If we don't have a native integration for your specific platform yet, don't worry. You can still get everything connected using **Webhooks**.

<Tip>You can set up your conversions via **Zapier**, **Make.com**, **n8n**, or even a native webhook if your platform supports it. This ensures no matter what your tech stack looks like, we can track it.</Tip>

Go ahead and get those integrations connected, and away you go!
