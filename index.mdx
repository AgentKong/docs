## What can you build?

- **AI Business Assistant** — Ask Claude about your revenue, ad performance, churn, and customer journeys
- **Custom Dashboards** — Pull attribution, Stripe, and Shopify data into your own tools
- **Automated Workflows** — Trigger actions based on conversations, conversions, or subscription events
- **Customer Intelligence** — Analyze contact journeys from first ad click through purchase and beyond
